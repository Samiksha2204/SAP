REPORT  Z15PROG10.

PARAMETERS: P_DATE TYPE SY-DATUM OBLIGATORY DEFAULT SY-DATUM.
START-OF-SELECTION.
PERFORM DO_ENDDO.
END-OF-SELECTION.


FORM DO_ENDDO.
  DO.
  WRITE:/ 'DATE',P_DATE ,SY-TABIX.
  IF P_DATE > SY-DATUM.
    EXIT.
  ENDIF.
  ENDDO.

  P_DATE = P_DATE + 1.

  ENDFORM.
